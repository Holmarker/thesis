## Positioning notes

- **Nothing recent does what this thesis does**: a 28-league, monthly,
  ratings-based panel with player FE covering both the performance and
  playing-time margins. Closest published: PLOS One 2019 (event window, small N)
  and Deutscher & Büschemann (one league, Kicker ratings).
- **The AEL 2026 NBA result (null contract-year, post-signing dip) corroborates
  the headline across sport, data, and method** — cite in discussion, not just
  lit review.
- The lit-review arc that writes itself: Holmström signal → Gibbons–Murphy
  horizon → sports tests (mixed, measure-dependent: Krautmann, Berri–Krautmann)
  → football neighbors → non-sports analogues (probation, tenure, CEOs, term
  limits) → this thesis: better measure, bigger panel, both margins.
